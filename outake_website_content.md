### Artist Packages

**Section label:** Artist Packages

**Intro copy:**
Three tiers built around where you are as an artist. Whether you're defining your sound and image for the first time, or you're ready to step on a stage, there's a path for you here.

---

**Tier 1 — Foundation**

Tagline: For artists who are building their identity and need a strong, clear starting point.

Includes:
- Brand identity development (artist name, visual direction, logo/wordmark)
- Artist bio and press kit copy
- Social media profile setup and content direction
- 1-on-1 artist coaching sessions
- Image and positioning strategy

---

**Tier 2 — Momentum** *(Most Popular)*

Tagline: For artists ready to show the world who they are with content that actually moves people.

Includes everything in Foundation, plus:
- Music video production (concept, shoot, edit)
- Promotional photo and video shoot
- Social media content package (reels, clips, graphics)
- Campaign rollout strategy

---

**Tier 3 — Full Circle**

Tagline: For artists who are ready to perform. The full Outake experience from identity to stage.

Includes everything in Momentum, plus:
- Live performance booking and coordination
- Event production support
- Filmed and recorded performance (intimate session format)
- Post-show recap content and distribution

**Footer note:**
All packages are tailored to each artist. If you're not sure which tier fits where you are, reach out and we'll figure it out together.

### Brand Packages

**Section label:** Brand Packages

**Intro copy:**
For brands that already exist and want to look the part. Whether you need your visuals tightened up or a full content and campaign operation, we have a level for where you are.

---

**Tier 1 — Presence**

Tagline: For brands that need their visuals and messaging to feel clean, consistent, and credible.

Includes:
- Brand audit and visual identity review
- Logo refinement or refresh
- Color, typography, and visual standards guide
- Brand voice and messaging document
- Social media profile and bio alignment

---

**Tier 2 — Polish** *(Most Popular)*

Tagline: For brands ready to show up consistently with content that reflects the quality of what they do.

Includes everything in Presence, plus:
- Commercial or brand film production
- Branded photo and video shoot
- Social media content package (reels, graphics, promos)
- Content calendar and posting strategy

---

**Tier 3 — Platform**

Tagline: For brands that want to own their space, show up at events, and build a real audience around what they do.

Includes everything in Polish, plus:
- Event presence coordination and production
- Full social media campaign build and launch
- Custom branded merchandise (designed and printed in-house)
- Campaign reporting and performance recap

**Footer note:**
Every brand engagement starts with a conversation. We look at what you have, what you need, and build from there. No cookie-cutter work.

### Event Package

**Section label:** Event Package

**Intro copy:**
Live music is what we know. If you need a show planned, produced, and captured from start to finish, this is what we built for.

---

**Package Name: The Live Experience**

Tagline: A full-service package covering every phase of your live music event, from the first planning conversation to the content that lives on after the night is over.

**Phase 1 — Before the Show:**
- Venue scouting and coordination
- Artist booking and lineup curation
- Event branding and visual identity
- Promotional content and social campaign
- Ticketing and event logistics support

**Phase 2 — Day of the Show:**
- Full event production and setup
- Sound and stage coordination
- Live filming and photography
- On-site team and crew management
- Show flow and run of show direction

**Phase 3 — After the Show:**
- Highlight reel and event recap edit
- Edited photo gallery delivery
- Post-event social content package
- Intimate recorded artist moment (optional)
- Event recap and performance summary

**CTA:** Book a consultation

---

- Event Package uses a three-phase layout (before / day of / after) rather than a flat list.
- Add-ons section sits below the main Event Package card.
