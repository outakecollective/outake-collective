About Outake

A creative hub built for artists and brands who are ready to move with intention.

Outake is built for artists and brands who are ready to move with intention. Come in with a vision. Leave with everything you need to execute it.

Our Story

Outake was founded by three people who each saw the same gap from different angles: a graphic designer, a social worker, and a film major. Different disciplines, one shared belief that the creative process should feel as good as the final product.

We built Outake to be more than a service. It's a collective, a space where the work is personal, the team is invested, and the output is something you're genuinely proud to put your name on.

"An experience people enjoy during and after." That's been the standard from day one and it still is.
