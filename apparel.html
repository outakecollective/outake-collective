The Process

Every piece starts as an idea. We design it, refine it, and bring it to life.

Outake Collective apparel is fully designed in-house by our creative team — from concept sketches to final print files. We partner with trusted print houses to execute each piece with the quality and attention to detail our community deserves. No mass production. No fast fashion. Every drop is limited and available exclusively at our popup events.

How It's Made

01
Concept & Design

Every piece originates from within the collective. Our in-house designers develop the full visual identity for each drop — from typography to colorways.

02
Print Production

We collaborate with carefully selected print houses to bring our designs to life with premium materials and techniques that hold up on and off the stage.

03
Limited Drops

No restocks. No online store. Each collection is available exclusively at Outake popup events — keeping the culture tied to the community.
