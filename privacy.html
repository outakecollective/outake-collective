Outake Collective ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website outakecollective.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site or interact with us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, email address, and any message you send through our contact form.

Any information you submit through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

If you contact us via our contact form or email, we will only use your email address to respond to your message. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

We take reasonable precautions to protect any information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use caution when sharing personal information online.

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our site, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.
