Philosophy

We don't take on clients. We build partnerships.

Every collaboration in Outake's orbit is rooted in shared values. We choose to work with brands, organizations, and creatives who believe in authentic work and the power of community — and are willing to trust the process to get there.

Community Partners

ByLAForLA
Health & Community Safety

By LA For LA is an evidence-based strategy addressing the overdose crisis by collaborating with community-based partners to provide health and social services across LA County. They provide resources and sterile medical devices — Narcan, snort cards, clean syringes — to Outake so that every event we produce is a safe space for our community.
